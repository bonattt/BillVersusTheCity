
## Weapons

### Real Gun Analogues

AR-15
AR-15 with bumpstock
Glock

### Categories

Assault Rifle [.223 Remington]
    - Accurate and deadly
    - high rate of semi-auto fire
    - decent armor penetration
    - good ammo capacity

    - slow to aim initially

Concealed Carry [9mm]
    - always equipped
    - fast to aim
    - low damage


Side Arm [9mm parabellums]
    - similar to concealed carry;
        - supports attachments
        - higher ammo capacity


Shotgun [12 gauge]:
    - extreme damage
    - slow RoF
    - poor against armor


Hunting Rifle [.30-06]:
    - extreme damage
    - extreme accuracy
    - slow slow rate of fire
    - slow to aim
    - scope
    - extreme armor penetration

    
### Weapon Stats

- rate of aim: how long is the time between starting to aim, and reaching max accuracy
- initial aim: how accurate is the gun when you start aiming
- accuracy: how accurate is the gun when fully aimed

- recoil_accuracy: how much is aim reduced by each shot
- recoil_shake: how much does point of aim move when gun shoots

- ammo type: type of ammo the gun uses [9mm, .223, .30-06]
- damage: damage dealt to target
- projectile_velocity

- semi_auto_fire_rate: how quickly can single shots be fired repeatedly
- full_auto_fire_rate: how quickly does fully auto shoot

- ammo_capacity: how many rounds are stored a fully loaded gun
- reload_amount: how many rounds are loaded by a reload action
- reload_time: how long does it take to perform a reload
